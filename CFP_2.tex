challenges in healthcare, agriculture, energy, urban development, finance, e-commerce, and
information security. By fostering interdisciplinary dialogue, the conference seeks to promote
innovative applications that enhance quality of life. Participants are invited to contribute to AI-
driven societal advancement. &  \\
